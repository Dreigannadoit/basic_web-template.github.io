My Proposal

Problem Statement

Traditional dental clinic booking systems are often inefficient, leading to long wait times, scheduling conflicts, and poor patient experience. Manual appointment management creates administrative burden and reduces clinic efficiency.

Solution Description

LMAO Clinical Booking is a comprehensive digital platform that automates appointment scheduling, patient management, and clinic operations. Our solution reduces administrative workload by 60% and improves patient satisfaction through seamless booking experiences and intelligent scheduling algorithms.

Better And Efficient

The system aims to enhance patient experience, reduce administrative workload, and improve overall clinic efficiency through automation and intelligent scheduling.






For Patients

  • Easy Appointment Booking
    Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments with just a few taps.
  • Dentist Directory
    View dentist profiles, specialties, and availability.
  • Real-time Notifications
    Receive SMS, email, or in-app reminders for upcoming appointments.
  • Digital Dental Records
    Access past treatments, prescriptions, and medical notes securely.
  • Online Consultation
    Video call or chat feature for quick dental advice or follow-ups.
  • Feedback & Ratings
    Leave reviews to help improve service quality.
  • Payment Integration
    Pay consultation or treatment fees via credit card, GCash, PayPal, etc.

For Clinics & Dentists

  • Smart Schedule Management
    View, approve, or modify patient bookings in real-time.
  • Patient Record System
    Maintain complete patient histories, including images and notes.
  • Automated Reminders
    Reduce no-shows with automated appointment reminders.
  • Analytics Dashboard
    Track appointments, revenue, and clinic performance.
  • Staff Management
    Assign tasks, monitor attendance, and manage multiple branches.
  • Inventory Tracking
    Monitor supplies and set alerts for low-stock dental materials.
  • Data Security & Compliance
    Ensure patient data is protected with encryption and access control.

Optional Add-ons

  • Loyalty & Rewards System
    Encourage patients to return through a point-based reward program.
  • AI-based Scheduling
    Optimize appointment times to reduce waiting periods.
  • Multi-language Support
    Cater to diverse patient groups.
  • Cloud Backup & Sync
    Securely store and access data across devices.
